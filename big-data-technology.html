Big Data Technology

With the technological advances of recent years, storage capacities and computing power have multiplied, becoming accessible for many companies.

Cloud computing, computer clusters and data lakes are concepts increasingly common in business today. Having real-time or near real-time results, with or without physical infrastructure, or having all your company’s data in a centralized and accessible location are examples of things that only recently were very difficult to imagine and now are readily available possibilities.

Kernel can assist you in choosing the appropriate technologies, as well as in their implementation, to maximize the competitiveness and efficiency of your business, and get you making better decisions based on real data.
